My Faviorte Player Jazz!

Jazz Chisholm Full Name: Jasrado Prince Hermis Arrington Chisholm Jr. Birthdate: February 1, 1998 Birthplace: Nassau, Bahamas

Batting Avg .256

Chisholm is from Nassau, Bahamas. He was drawn to baseball by his grandmother, Patricia Coakley, who played shortstop for the Bahamian national softball team; she taught him to hit at two years old. Chisholm moved to the United States at 12 years old to attend high school at Life Prep Academy in Wichita, Kansas.
